About Me

I am a full stack developer with a background in engineering, photography, and a lifetime of problem solving. My unique experiences allow me to approach assignments with the precision of a scientist, the creativity of an artist, and the tenacity of a practiced trouble shooter. Fortunately, my problem-solving doggedness doesn't overshadow my easy-going nature and eagerness to both learn and help others.

When I am not working at the computer or camera, you can find me with my family. A bit of a geek by nature, I play RPG and board games, appreciate the finer points of certain fandoms, and enjoy watching my kids go "totally fan girl" at Comicons. However, I also practice more traditional pursuits of tennis, knitting, calligraphy, and playing my viola in a local orchestra.

Currently, I am engaged in the University of Texas Coding Boot Camp to learn the skills to become a full stack developer. I will be graduating in April 2017 and would love to find a position that allows me to build on these techniques to solve problems and help others. Want to work together? I'd love to hear from you!
